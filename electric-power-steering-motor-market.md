<p><strong>The Electric Power Steering Motor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CEPS Motor</li><li>REPS Motor</li><li>PEPS Motor</li><li>EHPS Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>- CEPS Motor: This type of motor is known as Column Electric Power Steering Motor, which is commonly used in vehicles for steering assist.</p><p>- REPS Motor: This type is known as Rack Electric Power Steering Motor, which is integrated into the steering rack to provide power assistance.</p><p>- PEPS Motor: This type is known as Pinion Electric Power Steering Motor, which is mounted on the steering column to provide steering assistance.</p><p>- EHPS Motor: This type is known as Electric Hydraulic Power Steering Motor, which combines electric and hydraulic technology for steering assist in vehicles.</p></p>
