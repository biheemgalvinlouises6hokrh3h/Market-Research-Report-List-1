<p><p>Resin Fillers are categorized into two main types: Glass Filler and Inorganic Filler. Glass Fillers are made from finely ground glass particles, which offer high strength and durability to the resin. Inorganic Fillers, on the other hand, are composed of various minerals such as calcium carbonate or aluminum hydroxide, providing benefits like improved thermal and chemical resistance. Both types of fillers play a crucial role in enhancing the overall performance and properties of resin-based materials in various industries.</p></p>
