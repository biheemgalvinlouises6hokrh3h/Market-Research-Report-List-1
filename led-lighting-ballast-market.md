<p><strong>The LED Lighting Ballast Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Constant-current Drivers</li><li>Constant-voltage Drivers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>LED Lighting Ballast Market Types include Constant-current Drivers and Constant-voltage Drivers. Constant-current Drivers regulate the electric current flowing through the LED, ensuring a consistent level of current regardless of fluctuations in voltage. They are ideal for applications where maintaining a steady current is crucial for optimal performance. Constant-voltage Drivers, on the other hand, provide a fixed voltage output to the LED, making them suitable for situations where a stable voltage is required. Both types play a critical role in maximizing the efficiency and lifespan of LED lighting systems.</p></p>
