<p><p>Servohydraulic test systems are divided into two main market types: dynamic cyclic testing and static testing. Dynamic cyclic testing involves subjecting materials or components to repetitive loading and unloading cycles to simulate real-world conditions. This type of testing is essential for assessing fatigue life and durability. On the other hand, static testing involves applying a constant load or force to evaluate the strength and stiffness of materials. Both market types play a crucial role in product development, quality control, and research in various industries.</p></p>
