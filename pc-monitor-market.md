<p><p>The PC monitor market consists of two main types of displays: LCD and OLED. LCD monitors use liquid crystal technology to display images, providing high resolution and color accuracy. On the other hand, OLED monitors use organic light-emitting diodes to produce images with deeper blacks and vibrant colors.
