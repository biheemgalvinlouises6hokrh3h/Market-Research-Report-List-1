<p><p>The superhard cutting tool market can be divided into two main types: polycrystalline cubic boron nitride (PCBN) and polycrystalline diamond (PCD). PCBN tools are known for their high hardness and thermal stability, making them ideal for cutting hard materials like hardened steel. On the other hand, PCD tools are prized for their exceptional wear resistance and low friction properties, making them suitable for cutting non-ferrous metals and abrasive materials. Both types of superhard cutting tools offer superior performance and durability in various machining applications.</p></p>
