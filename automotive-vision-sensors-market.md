<p><strong>The Automotive Vision Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monocular Vision</li><li>Binocular (stereo) Vision</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive vision sensors are essential components in advanced driver assistance systems. Monocular vision sensors use a single camera to capture images of the road ahead, while binocular (stereo) vision sensors utilize two cameras to provide depth perception and enhance accuracy in object detection. Monocular vision sensors are cost-effective and widely used, while binocular vision sensors offer improved depth perception and better performance in complex driving scenarios. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing driver safety and enabling autonomous driving technology.</p></p>
