<p><p>Passive hydroponics systems market includes various types such as hydroponic drip systems, flood & drain systems, N.F.T. (Nutrient Film Technique), and water culture hydroponic systems. Hydroponic drip systems involve dripping nutrient solution directly onto plant roots. Flood & drain systems flood plants with nutrient solution before draining it away. N.F.T. systems circulate a thin film of nutrient solution along the roots of plants. Water culture hydroponic systems suspend plant roots in a nutrient-rich solution. Each type offers unique benefits for growing plants hydroponically.</p></p>
