<p><p>Mineral wool insulation is divided into two main types: glass wool and stone wool. Glass wool is made from recycled glass and is known for its excellent thermal and acoustic properties. Stone wool, on the other hand, is made from volcanic rock and is known for its high fire resistance. Both types of mineral wool insulation provide energy efficiency, soundproofing, and fire protection for buildings. The market for these products is driven by the growing demand for sustainable and eco-friendly building materials.</p></p>
