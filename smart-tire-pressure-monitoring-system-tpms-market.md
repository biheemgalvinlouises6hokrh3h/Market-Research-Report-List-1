<p><p>The Smart Tire Pressure Monitoring System (TPMS) market consists of two main types: Indirect TPMS and Direct TPMS. Indirect TPMS uses the ABS system to monitor tire pressure, while Direct TPMS uses sensors within each tire to provide real-time pressure data. Indirect TPMS is typically less expensive but may be less accurate, while Direct TPMS offers more precise monitoring but at a higher cost. Both systems help improve vehicle safety and fuel efficiency by ensuring optimal tire pressure levels.</p></p>
