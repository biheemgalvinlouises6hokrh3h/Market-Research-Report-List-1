<p><strong>The PET Film Release Liner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Silicone Film</li><li>Non-Silicone Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PET Film Release Liner market can be classified into two main types: Silicone Film and Non-Silicone Film. Silicone Film release liners are coated with a layer of silicone, providing superior release properties for adhesive materials. On the other hand, Non-Silicone Film release liners do not contain silicone and are often preferred for applications where silicone contamination is a concern. Both types of release liners play a crucial role in various industries such as electronics, medical, and labeling for protecting adhesive materials during storage and transportation.</p></p>
