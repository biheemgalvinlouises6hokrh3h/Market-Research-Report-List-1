<p><strong>The Epoxy Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Liquid</li><li>Solid</li><li>Solution</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The epoxy market is divided into three main types: liquid epoxy, solid epoxy, and epoxy solutions. Liquid epoxy is a versatile form that can be easily applied and molded before hardening. Solid epoxy comes in a preformed solid state, often in the form of resins or adhesives. Epoxy solutions are mixtures of epoxy resins and various solvents or diluents, allowing for easier application and customization of the final product. Each type caters to different applications and preferences within the epoxy market.</p></p>
