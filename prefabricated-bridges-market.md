<p><p>Prefabricated bridges are categorized into different types based on their design and structure. Beam Type bridges are simple and commonly used for short spans. Arch Type bridges have a curved shape that allows for longer spans without needing additional supports. Suspension Type bridges use cables to hang the bridge deck and are suitable for long spans. Other types of prefabricated bridges may include truss bridges, cable-stayed bridges, and truss-girder bridges. Each type offers unique advantages and is designed to meet specific project requirements.</p></p>
