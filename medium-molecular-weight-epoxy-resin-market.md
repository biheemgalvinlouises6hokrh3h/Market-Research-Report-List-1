<p><p>Medium molecular weight epoxy resin is categorized into three types based on their viscosity levels - low, medium, and high viscosity epoxy resin. Low viscosity epoxy resin is fluid and easy to pour, making it ideal for applications where high penetration is required. Medium viscosity epoxy resin offers a balance between flow and resistance, suitable for general adhesive and coating applications. High viscosity epoxy resin is thick and provides excellent gap filling properties, making it ideal for structural bonding and composite applications.</p></p>
