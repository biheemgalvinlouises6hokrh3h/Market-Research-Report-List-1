<p><strong>The Electrically Conductive Epoxy Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Isotropic</li><li>Anisotropic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electrically conductive epoxy is available in two main types: isotropic and anisotropic. Isotropic electrically conductive epoxy has the same electrical conductivity properties in all directions, making it ideal for applications where uniform conductivity is required. On the other hand, anisotropic electrically conductive epoxy has different conductivity properties in different directions, making it suitable for applications where selective conductivity is needed, such as in printed circuit boards. Both types of electrically conductive epoxy offer excellent adhesion properties and can be used in a variety of industries.</p></p>
