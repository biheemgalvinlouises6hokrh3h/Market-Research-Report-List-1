<p><ul><li>Single Stage</li><li>Double Stage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive Speed Reducers are categorized into two main types - Single Stage and Double Stage. Single Stage reducers have a simpler design with one set of gears that reduce speed, making them more compact and cost-effective. Double Stage reducers have an additional set of gears that further reduce speed, providing higher torque output and better efficiency, but are larger and more expensive. Both types are used in various automotive applications to adapt the speed and torque output of the vehicle's engine to the desired level.</p></p>
