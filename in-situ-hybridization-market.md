<p><strong>The In-Situ Hybridization Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Radioactive isotopes</li><li>Non-radioactive labels</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In-Situ Hybridization market includes two main types: Radioactive isotopes and Non-radioactive labels. Radioactive isotopes are used for detecting gene sequences through radioactive decay, while Non-radioactive labels use enzymes or fluorescent dyes to visualize gene sequences. Both methods have their advantages and disadvantages. Radioactive isotopes offer high sensitivity but are hazardous, while Non-radioactive labels are safer but may have lower sensitivity. The choice between the two types depends on the specific needs and requirements of the research or diagnostic application.</p></p>
