Xanthan Gum is a common stabilizer used for its thickening and stabilizing properties. Carboxymethyl Cellulose is another stabilizer known for its ability to enhance the texture and mouthfeel of beverages. Carrageenan is a natural stabilizer derived from seaweed, offering gelling properties. Gum Arabic, sourced from the Acacia tree, is a versatile stabilizer used for its emulsifying and thickening properties in beverages.</p></p>
