<p><ul><li>Single Function</li><li>Multi-Function</li></ul></p>
<p><p>DC Voltage Transducers can be classified into two main types: Single Function and Multi-Function. Single Function transducers are designed to measure and convert DC voltage into a proportional output signal. On the other hand, Multi-Function transducers are capable of measuring multiple parameters such as current, temperature, and power in addition to DC voltage. Single Function transducers are typically more cost-effective and simpler to use, while Multi-Function transducers offer greater flexibility and versatility in monitoring multiple parameters simultaneously.</p></p>
