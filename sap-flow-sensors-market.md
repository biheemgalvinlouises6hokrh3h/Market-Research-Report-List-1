<p><p>Sap flow sensors are used to measure the flow of sap within plants, providing valuable information for researchers and farmers. There are three main types of sap flow sensors on the market: conventional sensors, thermopile sensors, and other specialized sensors. Conventional sensors use methods such as heat dissipation or heat pulse velocity to measure sap flow. Thermopile sensors use thermoelectric properties to monitor sap flow. Other specialized sensors may utilize different technologies or approaches to measure sap flow in specific plant species or environments.</p></p>
