It comes in different types, including needle coke, shot coke, sponge coke, honeycomb coke, and others. Needle coke is high-quality and used in the production of graphite electrodes. Shot coke is smaller and harder, suitable for fuel grinding. Sponge coke is porous and used in aluminum reduction. Honeycomb coke has a honeycomb-like structure, while other types cater to specific industry needs.</p></p>
