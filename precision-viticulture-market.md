<p><p>Precision Viticulture is a technology-driven approach that encompasses various market types to optimize vineyard management. Guidance System involves using GPS technology to navigate and track equipment in the vineyard. Remote Sensing utilizes satellites or drones to collect data on plant health and soil conditions. Variable-Rate Technology enables farmers to apply inputs such as water and fertilizers at varying rates based on specific vineyard conditions. These market types work together to enhance efficiency, productivity, and sustainability in vineyard operations.</p></p>
