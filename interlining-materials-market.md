<p><p>Interlining materials market can be categorized into two types: fusible interlining and non-fusible interlining. Fusible interlining is a type of material that bonds to the fabric using heat, providing structure and reinforcement to the garment. On the other hand, non-fuse interlining does not require heat for bonding and is sewn into the garment to provide support. Each type has its own advantages and uses in different applications within the textile industry.</p></p>
