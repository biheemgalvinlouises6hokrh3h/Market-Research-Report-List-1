<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The High-density Calcined Coke Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Needle Coke</li><li>Shot Coke</li><li>Sponge Coke</li><li>Honeycomb Coke</li></ul></p>
<p><p>High-density calcined coke is classified into four types based on its physical characteristics and production process. Needle coke has a high degree of anisotropy, making it ideal for manufacturing electrodes for the steel and aluminum industries. Shot coke is produced quickly at high temperatures, resulting in irregular shaped particles suitable for anode production. Sponge coke has a porous structure, making it effective for carbon products. Honeycomb coke has a unique cellular structure, making it valuable for carbon anodes in the aluminum industry.</p></p>
