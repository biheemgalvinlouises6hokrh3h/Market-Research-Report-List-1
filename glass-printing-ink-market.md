<p><p>Glass printing ink market is divided into two main types: Oily Glass Ink and Water Glass Ink. Oily Glass Ink is primarily oil-based, offering durability and resistance to fading. It is suitable for printing on glass surfaces such as bottles and jars. On the other hand, Water Glass Ink is water-based, making it eco-friendly and easy to clean. It is commonly used for printing on glassware like cups and plates. Both types of ink cater to different printing needs in the glass industry, providing options for various applications.</p></p>
