<p><p>Electrical sub panels are used to branch off from the main electrical panel to provide power to specific areas. Single phase electrical sub panels are designed for residential and light commercial use, while three phase electrical sub panels are typically used in industrial settings to meet heavier power demands. Single phase panels have one line and two load wires, while three phase panels have three lines and three load wires. Both types are important components in electrical systems for distributing power safely and efficiently.</p></p>
