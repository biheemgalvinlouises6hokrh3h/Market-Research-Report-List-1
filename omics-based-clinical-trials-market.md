<p><p>Omics-based clinical trials market types include interventional studies, observational studies, and expanded access studies. Interventional studies involve testing the effectiveness of a treatment or intervention on a specific group of participants. Observational studies observe and analyze participants without intervening in their treatment. Expanded access studies allow patients with serious or life-threatening conditions to access investigational drugs outside of clinical trials. These different types of studies play a crucial role in advancing medical research and improving patient outcomes.</p></p>
