<p><p>Semiconductor Silicon Epitaxial Wafer Market Types include P Type and N Type. P Type wafers have a predominant presence of positively charged holes, while N Type wafers have a predominant presence of negatively charged electrons. P Type wafers are used in applications requiring the movement of positively charged carriers, while N Type wafers are used in applications requiring the movement of negatively charged carriers. Both types of wafers play a crucial role in the semiconductor industry by enabling the fabrication of various electronic devices and components.</p></p>
