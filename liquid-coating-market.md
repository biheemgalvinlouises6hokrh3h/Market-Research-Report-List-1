<p><ul><li>Solvent Borne</li><li>Water Borne</li><li>UV Cure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Liquid coating market is primarily classified into three types: Solvent Borne, Water Borne, and UV Cure. Solvent Borne coatings contain volatile organic compounds, making them harmful to the environment. Water Borne coatings are eco-friendly and contain water as the primary solvent. UV Cure coatings use ultraviolet light to cure, offering a fast and efficient coating process. Each type has its advantages and disadvantages, with water borne and UV cure coatings gaining popularity due to their environmentally friendly nature and faster curing times.</p></p>
