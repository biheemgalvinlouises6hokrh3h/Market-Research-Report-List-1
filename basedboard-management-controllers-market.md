<p><p>Integrated Baseboard Management Controllers are embedded within the server motherboard, providing a cost-effective solution for managing server hardware. They are designed to work seamlessly with the server hardware and offer basic management functions. Independent Baseboard Management Controllers, on the other hand, are standalone devices that can be added to existing servers to provide more advanced management capabilities. They operate independently of the server hardware and can be customized to meet specific requirements. Both types cater to different needs in the server management market.</p></p>
