<p><p>A multi-function oscilloscope market consists of two main types: digital oscilloscopes and analog oscilloscopes. Digital oscilloscopes use analog-to-digital converters to capture waveforms and offer advanced features such as signal analysis and storage capabilities. They are preferred for their versatility and ease of use. On the other hand, analog oscilloscopes use cathode ray tubes to display waveforms in real-time and are known for their simplicity and reliability. Both types cater to different user preferences and applications in the electronics industry.</p></p>
