<p><p>Packaging adhesives market consists of three main types: water-based, solvent-based, and hot-melt based adhesives. </p><p>Water-based adhesives are environmentally friendly and easy to clean up, making them increasingly popular in packaging applications.</p><p>Solvent-based adhesives offer strong bonds and fast curing times, but their volatile organic compounds pose environmental and health risks.</p><p>Hot-melt based adhesives provide quick bonding and high initial strength, ideal for high-speed packaging operations.</p><p>Each type of adhesive offers unique benefits and characteristics to cater to different packaging needs.</p></p>
