<p><p>Heavy Duty Safety Switch market types include Fusible Disconnect and Non-fusible Disconnect options. Fusible Disconnect switches have fuses that provide overload protection by interrupting the circuit when necessary. Non-fusible Disconnect switches do not have fuses, but still offer a safe way to disconnect power in case of emergencies. Both types are essential for ensuring safety in industrial settings, but they differ in their methods of protection. Customers can choose the type that best suits their specific needs and requirements.</p></p>
