<p><p>The Aerial Work Platform (AWP) Truck market consists of various types of lifts, including Boom Lifts, Scissor Lifts, Vertical Mast Lifts, and Personnel Portable Lifts. Boom lifts have a telescopic arm that allows for vertical and horizontal reach. Scissor lifts have a crisscrossing mechanism that extends vertically. Vertical Mast Lifts have a single mast that extends vertically. Personnel Portable Lifts are smaller platforms that can be easily moved around. Each type of lift serves a specific purpose in various industries for elevated work tasks.</p></p>
