<p><p>The Exercise Oxygen Equipment market includes oxygen concentrators, compressed gas systems, and liquid oxygen systems. Oxygen concentrators are devices that extract oxygen from the air and deliver it to patients, while compressed gas systems store oxygen in tanks for portable use. Liquid oxygen systems store oxygen in liquid form and convert it to gas for inhalation. Each type of equipment is utilized in different settings to provide oxygen therapy to individuals with respiratory conditions during exercise or physical activity.</p></p>
