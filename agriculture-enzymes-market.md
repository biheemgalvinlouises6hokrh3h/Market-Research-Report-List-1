<p><p>Agriculture enzymes market types include Phosphatases, Dehydrogenases, Proteases, and Sulfatases. Phosphatases are enzymes that help in breaking down phosphate molecules in soil, aiding in nutrient absorption by plants. Dehydrogenases play a crucial role in soil respiration and nutrient cycling. Proteases are involved in breaking down proteins in soil, facilitating nutrient release. Sulfatases help in the breakdown of sulfur-containing compounds in soil, promoting plant growth. These enzymes play a significant role in enhancing soil health and promoting agricultural productivity.</p></p>
