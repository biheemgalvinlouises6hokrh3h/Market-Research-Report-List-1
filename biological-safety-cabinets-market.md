<p><p>Biological Safety Cabinets (BSCs) are essential in laboratories to protect operators, environment, and samples from exposure to biohazards. There are three main classes of BSCs: Class I, Class II, and Class III. </p><p>Class I BSCs provide operator protection and minimize sample contamination. Class II BSCs offer both operator and environmental protection, with different subtypes providing additional features. Class III BSCs provide maximum protection by containing the sample within a gas-tight enclosure, with all operations carried out through glove ports.</p></p>
