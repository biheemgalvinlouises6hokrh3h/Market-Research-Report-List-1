<p><p>Moisture cure adhesives are divided into two main types based on their flexibility: rigid moisture cure adhesive and soft moisture cure adhesive. Rigid moisture cure adhesives are known for their high strength and durability, making them suitable for applications where a strong bond is required. On the other hand, soft moisture cure adhesives are more flexible and have good elongation properties, making them ideal for applications where flexibility is key, such as bonding materials with different coefficients of thermal expansion.</p></p>
