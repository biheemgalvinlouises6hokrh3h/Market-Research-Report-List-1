<p><p>Death care services market includes two major types - cremation and burial market. Cremation involves the process of converting a deceased person's body into ashes through burning, which is considered a more cost-effective and environmentally friendly option. Burial, on the other hand, involves the traditional method of burying the deceased's body in a designated cemetery plot. Both types have their own set of cultural, religious, and personal preferences, where individuals and families make choices based on factors such as cost, religious beliefs, environmental concerns, and personal traditions.</p></p>
