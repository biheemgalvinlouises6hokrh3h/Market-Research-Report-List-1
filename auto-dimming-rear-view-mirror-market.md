<p><p>The auto dimming rear view mirror market consists of two main types: outer auto dimming mirrors and inside auto dimming mirrors. Outer auto dimming mirrors automatically adjust their tint based on the brightness of headlights behind the vehicle, reducing glare for the driver. Inside auto dimming mirrors do the same, but for headlights reflecting off the rear windshield. Both types enhance driving safety by improving visibility at night and in bright sunlight, making for a more comfortable driving experience.</p></p>
