<p><p>Pet bike carriers come in two main types, front-mounted and rear-mounted. Front-mounted carriers attach to the handlebars of the bike and allow the pet to ride in front of the rider, providing close interaction and visibility. Rear-mounted carriers attach to the back of the bike and provide a more stable and secure location for the pet to ride. Both types offer their own advantages, with front carriers emphasizing interaction and rear carriers focusing on stability and safety.</p></p>
