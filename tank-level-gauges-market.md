<p><p>Tank level gauges are used to measure the level of liquid in tanks. Different types of tank level gauges include magnetic, reflexive, transparent, bi-color, and others. Magnetic gauges use floatation principle, reflexive gauges measure level using reflections, transparent gauges provide direct visual indication, and bi-color gauges use two colors to represent liquid level. Other types may include ultrasonic and radar gauges. Each type offers unique features and benefits for accurate and reliable measurement of tank levels in various industries.</p></p>
