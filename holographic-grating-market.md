<p><strong>The Holographic Grating Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Plane Type Holographic Grating</li><li>Concave Type Holographic Grating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Plane type holographic gratings are flat, smooth surfaces with evenly spaced lines that diffract light in a specific pattern. They are commonly used in spectroscopy and telecommunications. Concave type holographic gratings have a curved surface that focuses diffracted light onto a specific point, making them ideal for applications requiring high spectral resolution. Both types of holographic gratings play a crucial role in various industries, including scientific research, imaging, and astronomy, providing unique capabilities for manipulating and analyzing light.</p></p>
