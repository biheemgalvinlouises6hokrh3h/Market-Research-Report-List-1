<p><p>GaAs attenuators are used in both super high frequency (SHF) and ultra high frequency (UHF) markets for signal attenuation purposes. SHF attenuators are designed for frequencies ranging from 3 to 30 GHz, typically used in radar systems, satellite communication, and medical equipment. Both markets require high-quality GaAs attenuators to ensure efficient signal management and transmission.</p></p>
