<p><p>Electronically Commutated (EC) Fans market consists of various types such as Centrifugal Fans, Axial Fans, Mixed Flow Fans, and Cross Flow Fans. Centrifugal Fans are commonly used in HVAC systems, industrial applications, and air purification systems. Axial Fans are ideal for applications requiring high airflow and low pressure. Mixed Flow Fans combine features of both centrifugal and axial fans, offering a balance between airflow and pressure. Cross Flow Fans are typically used in appliances like refrigerators and air conditioners for a uniform air distribution.</p></p>
