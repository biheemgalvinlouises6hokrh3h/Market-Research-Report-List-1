<p><p>Web sling market types include eye to eye type and endless type. Eye to eye type slings have loops on each end for easy attachment to hooks or other lifting devices. They are versatile and commonly used for general lifting applications. Endless type slings have no seams, making them more durable and less prone to wear and tear. They are preferred for heavier loads or when a more secure hold is needed. Both types are essential tools for lifting and rigging operations in various industries.</p></p>
