<p><p>Cold work die steel is used in various types of tooling applications such as blanking die, cold upsetting die, cold extrusion die, bending die, and drawing die. Blanking die is used for cutting or punching out shapes from sheet metal, while cold upsetting die is used for shaping metal through compressive force. Cold extrusion die is used for forming metal into complex shapes without heating. Bending die is used for shaping metal by bending it, and drawing die is used for pulling metal through a die to create a desired shape.</p></p>
