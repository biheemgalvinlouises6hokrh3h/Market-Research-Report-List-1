<p><strong>The GPS Anti-Jamming System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Nulling</li><li>Beam Steering Systems</li><li>Civilian Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>GPS anti-jamming systems are designed to protect GPS signals from intentional interference. There are three main types of systems in the market: nulling, beam steering, and civilian systems. Nulling systems work by canceling out jamming signals, while beam steering systems adjust the direction of the antenna to avoid interference. Civilian systems are designed for non-military applications and focus on protecting commercial users from jamming and spoofing attacks. These different types cater to a wide range of users with varying needs for GPS signal protection.</p></p>
