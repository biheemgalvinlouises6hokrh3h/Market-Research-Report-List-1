<p><p>Graphite sheets are primarily classified into three main types: Natural Graphite Sheet, Synthetic Graphite Sheet, and Nanocomposite Graphite Sheet. Natural Graphite Sheets are sourced from natural graphite ore, offering high thermal conductivity. Synthetic Graphite Sheets are produced through artificial processes to deliver specific performance characteristics. Nanocomposite Graphite Sheets consist of a combination of graphite and other nanomaterials, enhancing their mechanical and thermal properties. Each type serves unique applications based on their distinct properties and advantages in various industries.</p></p>
