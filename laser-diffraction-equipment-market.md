<p><p>Laser diffraction equipment market offers three main types for particle analysis: wet particle analysis, dry particle analysis, and spray particle analysis. Wet particle analysis is used for measuring particles suspended in liquid, such as in food or pharmaceutical applications. Dry particle analysis is used for measuring solid particles in powder or granular form. Spray particle analysis is used for studying particles generated by spray systems, such as in aerosols or sprays. Each type of analysis caters to specific industries and applications for precise particle size measurement.</p></p>
