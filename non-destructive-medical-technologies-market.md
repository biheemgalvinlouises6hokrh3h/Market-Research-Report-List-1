<p><p>Non-Destructive Medical Technologies are used to examine the internal structures of the human body without causing harm. Echocardiography uses sound waves to create images of the heart. Medical Ultrasonography employs high-frequency sound waves to produce images of organs and tissues within the body. Liquid Penetrant testing detects surface-breaking defects in materials. Phased Array Ultrasonic Testing uses multiple ultrasonic beams for detailed imaging. Digital Radiography captures X-ray images digitally for quick analysis. These technologies are vital in diagnosing and monitoring various medical conditions.</p></p>
