<p><p>Polyolefin ceramic coated separators are a key component in lithium-ion batteries, providing thermal stability and preventing short-circuiting. The two main types of polyolefin ceramic coated separators are PP type and PE type. PP type separators are made from polypropylene, offering high thermal stability and chemical resistance. PE type separators, on the other hand, are made from polyethylene and provide better mechanical strength and flexibility. Both types of separators play a crucial role in enhancing the performance and safety of lithium-ion batteries.</p></p>
