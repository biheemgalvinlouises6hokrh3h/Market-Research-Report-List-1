<p><p>A vehicle supercharger is a device that helps increase the power output of an internal combustion engine by compressing the intake air before it enters the engine cylinders. This allows for more air and fuel to be burned in each cycle, resulting in increased horsepower and torque. Vehicle superchargers are commonly used in high-performance cars, trucks, and motorcycles to enhance engine performance.</p><p>The global vehicle supercharger market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.8% during the forecast period. The growing demand for high-performance vehicles, as well as the need for fuel efficiency and lower emissions, are driving the market growth. Increasing consumer preference for enhanced driving experience and improved vehicle performance is also contributing to the market expansion.</p><p>The latest trends in the vehicle supercharger market include the development of electric superchargers, which offer improved efficiency and faster response times compared to traditional superchargers. Additionally, advancements in technology such as variable-speed superchargers and integrated systems are further driving market growth. The increasing adoption of superchargers in SUVs and trucks for improved towing capacity and off-road performance is also a key trend in the market.</p></p>
