<p><strong>The Pipeline Basket Strainers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Simplex Basket Strainers</li><li>Duplex Basket Strainers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pipeline Basket Strainers are available in two main types: Simplex Basket Strainers and Duplex Basket Strainers. Simplex Basket Strainers are designed for applications where temporary interruption of flow can be tolerated for strainer cleaning. Duplex Basket Strainers, on the other hand, are used for continuous operation as they have two separate chambers allowing for uninterrupted flow during maintenance. Both types are crucial in removing solid particles from the fluid flowing through pipelines to protect downstream equipment and ensure smooth operation.</p></p>
