<p><p>Proportional solenoid valves are used in the medical market for precise control of flow rates. Direct acting proportional solenoid valves operate by directly opening or closing the valve based on the input signal. Pilot proportional solenoid valves, on the other hand, work by controlling the flow of a pilot fluid which then regulates the main flow. Both types are crucial in medical applications where accurate and consistent flow control is necessary for tasks such as drug delivery or ventilation.</p></p>
