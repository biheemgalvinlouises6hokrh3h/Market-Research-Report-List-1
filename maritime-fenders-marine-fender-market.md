<p><p>Maritime fenders, also known as marine fenders, are crucial in protecting vessels and structures from collisions during docking or mooring operations. There are three primary types of maritime fenders: Solid Rubber Fenders, Pneumatic Fenders, and Foam Fenders. Solid Rubber Fenders are durable and provide reliable protection against impacts. Pneumatic Fenders, filled with compressed air, offer excellent energy absorption and are ideal for larger vessels. Foam Fenders, made of closed-cell foam, are lightweight, resilient, and require little maintenance. Each type has its unique advantages, ensuring a safe and secure maritime environment.</p></p>
