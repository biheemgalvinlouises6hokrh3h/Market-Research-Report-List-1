<p><p>Automated Peritoneal Dialysis Machines are classified into two main types: Power Type and Gravity Type. Power Type machines use an external power source to control the flow of dialysis solution, allowing for more precise control over the treatment process. Gravity Type machines rely on gravity to regulate the flow of the dialysis solution, making them more portable and requiring less maintenance. Both types offer effective treatment for patients with kidney failure, with each type having its own advantages and considerations.</p></p>
