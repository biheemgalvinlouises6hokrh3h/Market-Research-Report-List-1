<p><strong>The Industrial Panel PC Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fan-enabled Panel PC</li><li>Fanless Panel PC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial Panel PCs are commonly available in two main types: fan-enabled Panel PCs and fanless Panel PCs. Fan-enabled Panel PCs come equipped with cooling fans to regulate temperature levels and prevent overheating, making them suitable for applications in environments with high heat levels. On the other hand, fanless Panel PCs are designed without cooling fans, relying on passive cooling methods such as heat sinks to dissipate heat. Fanless Panel PCs are ideal for environments where noise, dust, and vibration could be an issue, as they offer silent operation and require less maintenance.</p></p>
