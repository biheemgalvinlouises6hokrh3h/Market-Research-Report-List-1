<p><p>Holographic transparent films are categorized into types based on the material used in their production. BOPET films are known for their high tensile strength and dimensional stability, while BOPP films are preferred for their excellent clarity and printability. PVC films are valued for their durability and versatility. Other types of holographic transparent films may include materials like polystyrene or polycarbonate.</p></p>
