<p><p>Aerosol overcaps are used to protect the spray nozzle of aerosol products. Two common types of overcaps are spray through overcaps, which allow the user to spray the product without removing the overcap, and traditional "dust-cover" overcaps, which are designed to simply protect the nozzle from dust and debris. Spray through overcaps are typically used for products that require frequent application, while traditional overcaps are more common for products that are used less frequently. Both types play a crucial role in preserving the quality and functionality of aerosol products.</p></p>
