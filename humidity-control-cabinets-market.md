<p><p>Humidity control cabinets are used to store sensitive items that are susceptible to moisture damage. The market for these cabinets can be categorized into two types - manual and automatic. Manual humidity control cabinets require the user to adjust and monitor the humidity levels manually, while automatic cabinets have built-in sensors and systems that automatically regulate the humidity. Manual cabinets offer more flexibility and control to the user but require constant monitoring, while automatic cabinets provide hassle-free operation with minimal intervention, making them ideal for large-scale or continuous use.</p></p>
