<p><ul><li>Engineered</li><li>Solid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Nailed parquet floor market consists of two main types: engineered and solid. Engineered parquet floors are made of multiple layers of wood pressed together in a cross-grain pattern, providing extra stability and resistance to moisture. Solid parquet floors, on the other hand, are made from one solid piece of wood, offering a more traditional and timeless look. Both types of flooring are commonly installed by nailing them down to the subfloor, providing a durable and long-lasting solution for any space.</p></p>
