<p><ul><li>Non-Gapped Line Arrester (NGLA)</li><li>Externally Gapped Line Arrester (EGLA)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Line Surge Arresters (LSA) are important devices used for protecting electrical systems from voltage surges. There are two main types in the market. Non-Gapped Line Arresters (NGLA) do not have any visible gaps and are designed for compact installations. On the other hand, Externally Gapped Line Arresters (EGLA) have visible gaps and are used for high voltage applications where extended insulation is required. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring the safety and reliability of electrical systems.</p></p>
