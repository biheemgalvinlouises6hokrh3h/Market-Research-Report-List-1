<p><p>The automotive differential gear market can be classified into different types based on the mechanism used. The common types include planetary, rack and pinion, worm, helical, non-metallic, and others. Planetary differential gears are compact and efficient, while rack and pinion gears are known for their precise steering. Worm gears offer high torque capacity, helical gears provide smooth operation, and non-metallic gears are lightweight and corrosion-resistant. Other types of differential gears exist to cater to specific requirements in the automotive industry.</p></p>
