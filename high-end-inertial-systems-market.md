<p><p>High-End Inertial Systems include High-End Inertial Measurement Units (IMUs), High-End Accelerometers, and High-End Gyroscopes. IMUs are used to measure and report specific force, angular rate, and sometimes magnetic field data. High-End Accelerometers measure acceleration forces, while High-End Gyroscopes measure the rate of rotation around a particular axis. These systems are essential in applications where precise and reliable motion sensing is required, such as aerospace, defense, and autonomous vehicles.</p></p>
