<p><p>Organic Light Emitting Diode (OLED) Displays come in two main types: Active-Matrix Organic Light-Emitting Diode (AMOLED) and Passive Matrix OLED (PMOLED). AMOLED displays are known for their high resolution, faster refresh rates, and improved color accuracy due to each pixel being controlled individually. On the other hand, PMOLED displays are simpler and cheaper to produce, making them suitable for smaller devices like wearable technology. Both types of OLED displays offer vibrant colors, high contrast ratios, and energy efficiency, making them popular choices in the consumer electronics market.</p></p>
