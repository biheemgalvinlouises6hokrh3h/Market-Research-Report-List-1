<p><p>Land Mobile Radio (LMR) Market includes three main types based on frequency ranges - 40MHz – 174MHz (VHF), 200MHz – 512MHz (UHF), and 700MHz – 1000MHz (SHF). VHF radios are suitable for long-range communication with better penetration in buildings and foliage. UHF radios offer better connectivity in urban areas with more channels and less interference. SHF radios provide high data transfer rates for advanced communication needs. Each frequency range caters to different communication requirements and environments in the LMR market.</p></p>
