<p><ul><li>Peat waxes</li><li>Ozocerite</li><li>Montan wax</li><li>Ceresin waxes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mineral waxes are classified into different types based on their source and properties. Peat waxes are derived from peat moss, known for their high moisture retention and medicinal properties. Ozocerite is a natural wax found in sedimentary rock formations, used in various cosmetic and pharmaceutical applications. Montan wax is extracted from lignite coal, known for its high melting point and water resistance. Ceresin waxes are created by distilling ozokerite, commonly used in making candles, polishes, and cosmetics.</p></p>
