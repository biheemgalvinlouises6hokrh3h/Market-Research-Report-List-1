<p><p>CMOS Linear Image Sensor market is divided into two main types: Analog Sensor and Digital Sensor. Analog Sensors convert light into electrical signals that vary continuously over time, providing a continuous output. On the other hand, Digital Sensors convert light into discrete digital signals, providing a series of binary outputs. Analog Sensors offer better performance in low-light conditions and have a higher dynamic range, while Digital Sensors offer easier integration with digital devices and better noise performance. Both types cater to different user requirements and preferences in the market.</p></p>
