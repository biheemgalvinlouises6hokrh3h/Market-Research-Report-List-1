<p><p>Optical Fiber Preform market includes various types such as Vapor-phase Axial Deposition (VAD), Outside Vapor Deposition (OVD), Plasma Chemical Vapor Deposition (PCVD), and Modified Chemical Vapor Deposition (MCVD). VAD involves synthesizing glass particles through chemical reactions in vapor phase. OVD uses a rotating lathe to deposit glass layers on a rotating rod. PCVD uses plasma to deposit glass onto a substrate. MCVD involves using a torch to deposit glass inside a fused silica tube. Each method has its own advantages and applications in the optical fiber industry.</p></p>
