<p><p>Organic Vegetable Seeds are divided into Conventional Vegetable Seeds and Hybrid Vegetable Seeds markets. Conventional Vegetable Seeds are traditional, non-genetically modified seeds that are open-pollinated and produce plants with stable traits. On the other hand, Hybrid Vegetable Seeds are created by crossing two different parent plants to create a new, superior variety with specific desirable traits. Both markets cater to different consumer needs and preferences, with Conventional Vegetable Seeds appealing to those seeking organic, natural options and Hybrid Vegetable Seeds offering innovative solutions for improved quality and yield.</p></p>
