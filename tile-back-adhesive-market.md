<p><p>Tile back adhesive market consists of three main types: 1 Component Paste BG, 1 Component Liquid BG, and 2 Component BG. 1 Component Paste BG is a thick adhesive applied directly from the tube, offering convenience and ease of use. 1 Component Liquid BG is a thinner adhesive that can be easily spread with a trowel or roller. 2 Component BG involves mixing two separate components to activate the adhesive, providing a stronger and more durable bond. Each type caters to different preferences and project requirements in the construction industry.</p></p>
