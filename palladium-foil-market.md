One major factor driving this growth is the increasing demand for palladium-based catalysts in the chemical industry. The growing focus on sustainable and environment-friendly manufacturing processes is also promoting the adoption of palladium foil as a catalyst.</p><p>In addition, the expanding automotive sector is contributing to the growth of the palladium foil market.
