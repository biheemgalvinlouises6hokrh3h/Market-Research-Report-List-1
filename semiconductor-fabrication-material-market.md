<p><p>Semiconductor fabrication materials refer to the various substances and components used in the production of semiconductors. The main types of materials in this market include silicon wafers, which serve as the base for electronic circuits, as well as photomasks, which are used in the lithography process. Photoresists are light-sensitive materials applied on wafers to create circuit patterns. Wet chemicals are used for cleaning and etching purposes. CMP slurry and pads aid in planarization during the manufacturing process. Gases are essential for various fabrication steps, while sputter targets are used for physical vapor deposition. Additionally, there are photoresist ancillaries and other miscellaneous materials involved in semiconductor fabrication.</p></p>
