<p><ul><li>One-Component</li><li>Two-Component</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive Exterior Adhesives market can be divided into two main types based on the number of components in the adhesive formulation. One-component adhesives are pre-mixed and ready to use, making them convenient for quick application. On the other hand, two-component adhesives require mixing of two separate components before application, offering higher strength and durability. While one-component adhesives are easier to use, two-component adhesives provide better performance in demanding automotive exterior applications. Both types have their unique advantages and are used based on specific requirements.</p></p>
