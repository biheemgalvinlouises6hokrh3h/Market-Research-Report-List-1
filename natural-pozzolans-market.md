<p><p>Natural Pozzolans are classified into two main types: Raw Pozzolan and Calcined Pozzolan. Raw Pozzolan refers to natural materials like volcanic ash or pumice that have not undergone any additional processing. On the other hand, Calcined Pozzolan is produced through the heating and grinding of raw materials to improve their reactivity. Both types of pozzolans are used in construction materials like concrete to enhance strength, durability, and reduce environmental impacts. Each type offers unique characteristics and benefits to various applications in the construction industry.</p></p>
