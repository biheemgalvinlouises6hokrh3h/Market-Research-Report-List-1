<p><p>Contact type liquid level switch market consists of various types such as float liquid level switch, capacitive level switch, and others. Float liquid level switches operate with a float that rises or falls with the liquid level, triggering a switch to turn on or off. Capacitive level switches use changes in capacitance to detect liquid levels. Other types of contact liquid level switches include optical level switches, conductive level switches, and vibrating fork level switches. These switches are widely used in industries such as oil and gas, chemical, and water treatment for accurate liquid level monitoring.</p></p>
