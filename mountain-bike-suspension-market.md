<p><strong>The Mountain Bike Suspension Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Front Suspension</li><li>Rear Suspension</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mountain bike suspension can be categorized into two main types: front suspension and rear suspension. Front suspension, also known as a hardtail, features only a suspension fork in the front of the bike, offering more control when riding on rough terrain. Rear suspension, on the other hand, includes a shock absorber in addition to the front suspension fork, providing a smoother and more comfortable ride by absorbing impacts from both the front and rear wheels. Both types of suspension systems enhance the rider's overall experience and performance on various terrains.</p></p>
