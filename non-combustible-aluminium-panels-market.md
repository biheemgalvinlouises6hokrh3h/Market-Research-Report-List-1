<p><p>Non-combustible aluminium panels come in various types, including anodised and powder coated options. Anodised panels are treated with an electrolytic process to create a durable surface finish, making them resistant to corrosion and fading. Powder coated panels are coated with a dry powder that is then heated, creating a tough and scratch-resistant finish. Both types of panels are popular choices for applications where fire safety is a priority, such as in commercial buildings and high-rise structures.</p></p>
