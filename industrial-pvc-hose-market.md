<p><p>The Industrial PVC Hose market consists of three main types: PVC Non Reinforced Hoses, PVC Fibre Reinforced Hoses, and PVC Steel Wire Hoses. PVC Non Reinforced Hoses are flexible and lightweight, suitable for low-pressure applications. PVC Fibre Reinforced Hoses are reinforced with high-strength synthetic fibres for added durability, often used in medium-pressure applications. PVC Steel Wire Hoses are reinforced with a steel wire helix for increased strength, commonly used in high-pressure industrial applications where flexibility and durability are essential.</p></p>
