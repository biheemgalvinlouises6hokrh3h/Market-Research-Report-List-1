<p><p>Flooring materials market is divided into three types: resilient, non-resilient, and soft cover. Resilient flooring includes materials like vinyl, rubber, and linoleum that are flexible and provide some cushioning. Non-resilient flooring includes materials such as ceramic tile, hardwood, and laminate that are more rigid. Soft cover flooring includes materials like carpet and rugs that provide a soft, cushioned surface. Each type of flooring material offers different benefits and is suitable for different applications and preferences.</p></p>
