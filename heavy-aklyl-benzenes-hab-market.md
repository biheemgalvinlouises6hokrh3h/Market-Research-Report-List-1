<p><p>Heavy Alkyl Benzenes (HAB) are widely used in various industries, including lubricants, oilfield chemicals, and fuel additives due to their excellent properties. There are two main market types for HAB: HLAB (Heavy Linear Alkyl Benzene) and HBAB (Heavy Branched Alkyl Benzene). HLAB is known for its high thermal stability and low pour point, making it ideal for use in industrial lubricants. On the other hand, HBAB is preferred for its improved detergency and oxidation stability, making it suitable for use in detergent formulations.</p></p>
