<p><p>The Nylon 6 Filament Yarn market can be categorized into various types. Firstly, the Flat market refers to the production of nylon yarn with a uniform thickness. POY (Partially Oriented Yarn) refers to a type of yarn that has been partially stretched and provides good dyeability and stretchability. FDY (Fully Drawn Yarn) refers to yarn that has been fully stretched and has improved strength and abrasion resistance. Lastly, HSO (High-Speed Oriented) refers to yarn produced through a high-speed spinning process, resulting in enhanced tensile strength and smoothness.</p></p>
