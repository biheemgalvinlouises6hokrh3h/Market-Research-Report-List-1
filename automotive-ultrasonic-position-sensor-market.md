<p><strong>The Automotive Ultrasonic Position Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Discrete Ultrasonic Position Sensor</li><li>Continuous Ultrasonic Position Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Ultrasonic Position Sensor market can be categorized into two main types: Discrete Ultrasonic Position Sensor and Continuous Ultrasonic Position Sensor. Discrete Ultrasonic Position Sensors provide discrete outputs such as ON/OFF signals based on the presence or absence of an object within a certain range. On the other hand, Continuous Ultrasonic Position Sensors offer continuous feedback on the exact distance or position of an object in real-time. Both types play a crucial role in automotive applications for detecting objects and ensuring safety while driving.</p></p>
