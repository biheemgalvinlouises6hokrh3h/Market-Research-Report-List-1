<p><strong>The Ceramic Electrostatic Chuck for Semiconductor Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coulomb Type</li><li>Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ceramic Electrostatic Chuck for Semiconductor Equipment market types include Coulomb Type and Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type. Coulomb Type chucks utilize electrostatic attraction between two charged surfaces, allowing for secure wafer positioning. On the other hand, JR Type chucks incorporate a dielectric layer between the chuck and the wafer, resulting in improved chucking force uniformity. Both types offer benefits such as high holding force, uniform pressure distribution, and minimal contamination risks, making them popular choices in the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
