<p><ul><li>Single Layer</li><li>Double Layer</li><li>Multi-Layer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rigid-flex PCBs are available in three main types - single layer, double layer, and multi-layer. Single layer rigid-flex PCBs consist of a single layer of flexible circuit with a single layer of rigid board. Double layer rigid-flex PCBs have two layers of flexible circuits with two layers of rigid board. Multi-layer rigid-flex PCBs have multiple layers of flexible circuits sandwiched between multiple layers of rigid board. Each type offers different levels of complexity and design flexibility to suit various applications and requirements.</p></p>
