<p><p>The Dissolving Wood Cellulose (DWC) market includes two main types: Alkali Wood Pulp and Sulfate Wood Pulp. Alkali Wood Pulp is produced using a chemical process that involves sodium hydroxide, while Sulfate Wood Pulp is produced using sulfuric acid. On the other hand, the Wood Pulp of Sulfite market involves a process using sulfurous acid to break down the cellulose fibers in the wood. These different types of wood pulp are important raw materials for various industries such as textiles, pharmaceuticals, and food.</p></p>
