<p><p>RFID and Barcode readers for the semiconductor market are essential for tracking and managing inventory in the production process. There are two main types of readers: CCD method and laser method. CCD method readers use an array of light sensors to capture barcode information, while laser method readers use a laser beam to scan and read barcodes quickly and accurately. Both types of readers are commonly used in semiconductor manufacturing facilities to improve efficiency and accuracy in inventory management.</p></p>
