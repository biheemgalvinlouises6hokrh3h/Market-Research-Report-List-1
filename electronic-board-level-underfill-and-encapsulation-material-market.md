<p><strong>The Electronic Board Level Underfill and Encapsulation Material Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>No Flow Underfill</li><li>Capillary Underfill</li><li>Molded Underfill</li><li>Wafer level Underfill</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electronic board level underfill and encapsulation material market includes several types such as no flow underfill, capillary underfill, molded underfill, and wafer level underfill. No flow underfill is used for flip-chip applications, capillary underfill flows between the component and substrate, molded underfill is pre-applied during assembly, and wafer level underfill is applied at the wafer level before singulating the chips. Each type caters to specific requirements in terms of flow control, application method, and end-use application in the electronics industry.</p></p>
