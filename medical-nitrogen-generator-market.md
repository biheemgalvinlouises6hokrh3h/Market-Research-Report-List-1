<p><p>Medical nitrogen generators are used to produce high-purity nitrogen for various medical applications. There are three main types of nitrogen generation technologies commonly used in the medical industry: Pressure Swing Adsorption (PSA), Carbon Molecular Sieve (CMS), and Membrane. PSA generators use adsorption to separate nitrogen from other gases, CMS generators utilize molecular sieves to remove impurities, and membrane generators rely on selective permeation through membranes. Each type has its own unique advantages and is widely used in hospitals, clinics, and research facilities.</p></p>
