<p><p>Dual channel digital isolators are used to provide electrical isolation between two separate circuits while allowing digital signals to pass through. There are three main types of dual channel digital isolators: capacitive coupling, magnetic coupling, and Giant Magnetoresistive (GMR). Capacitive coupling isolators use capacitors to transmit signals, magnetic coupling isolators use transformers, and GMR isolators use a unique magnetic sensor technology. Each type has its own advantages and applications, making them suitable for various industries and electronic devices.</p></p>
