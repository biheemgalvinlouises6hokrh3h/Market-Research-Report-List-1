<p><strong>The Insulating Glass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Conventional Insulating Glass Units</li><li>Low-E Insulating Glass Units</li><li>Traditional Reflective Insulating Glass Units</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Insulating glass market offers three main types: Conventional Insulating Glass Units, which are made of two or more glass panes separated by a spacer filled with air or gas for insulation. Low-E Insulating Glass Units have a special coating to reduce heat transfer and improve energy efficiency. Traditional Reflective Insulating Glass Units have a reflective coating to control light and heat transmission. These different types cater to varying needs for insulation, energy efficiency, and light control in buildings.</p></p>
