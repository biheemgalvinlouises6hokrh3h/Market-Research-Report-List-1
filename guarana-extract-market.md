<p><p>Guarana extract is available in two main types: dry extract and fluid extract. Dry extract refers to a concentrated form of guarana that has been processed to remove moisture, resulting in a powder or granular form. This type is often used in various supplements and health products. On the other hand, fluid extract is a liquid form of guarana that has been extracted using solvents. This type is commonly used in beverages, such as energy drinks and teas. Both dry and fluid extracts offer the beneficial properties of guarana in different forms for consumers' convenience.</p></p>
