<p><p>Succulent plants are categorized into different market types based on their botanical families, including Aizoaceae, Cactaceae, Crassulaceae, Euphorbiaceae, and Apocynaceae. Aizoaceae succulents, also known as ice plants, are known for their colorful and daisy-like flowers. Cactaceae succulents are characterized by their spiny stems and ability to store water in their tissues. Crassulaceae succulents, like jade plants, have thick leaves that store water. Euphorbiaceae succulents have a milky sap and unique shapes. Apocynaceae succulents, such as crown-of-thorns, have thick stems and colorful flowers. Each market type offers a variety of unique and beautiful succulents for collectors and enthusiasts.</p></p>
