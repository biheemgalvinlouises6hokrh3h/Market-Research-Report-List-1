<p><p>Mechanical tappets are essential components in internal combustion engines, used to transfer motion from the camshaft to the valves. Two common types of mechanical tappets include flat tappets and roller tappets. Flat tappets have a flat surface that comes in contact with the camshaft lobe, while roller tappets have a roller on the end that reduces friction and wear. Both types have their own advantages and disadvantages in terms of performance, cost, and maintenance requirements, which determine their popularity in the market.</p></p>
