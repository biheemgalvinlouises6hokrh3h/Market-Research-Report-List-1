<p><p>Metallised Polypropylene Films (MPP Films) are widely used in packaging, capacitors, and decorative applications. Two common types of MPP Films are Metallised Blow Polypropylene Films and Metallised Cast Polypropylene Films. Metallised Blow Polypropylene Films are produced through the blow extrusion process, providing excellent barrier properties and high strength. On the other hand, Metallised Cast Polypropylene Films are produced using the cast extrusion process, offering superior optical properties and good printing capabilities. Both types of films are popular choices in various industries for their durability and versatility.</p></p>
