<p><p>Refrigerant dehumidifiers come in two main types: Tower Type and Rotor Type. Tower Type dehumidifiers are typically larger in size and are designed to stand upright like a tower. They are more suitable for larger areas or commercial spaces. Rotor Type dehumidifiers, on the other hand, are more compact and utilize a rotor to absorb moisture from the air. They are often used in residential settings or smaller rooms. Both types are effective at reducing humidity levels in the air.</p></p>
