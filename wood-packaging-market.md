<p><p>Wood packaging market consists of three main types: pallets, cases, and boxes. Pallets are flat structures used to transport goods, typically stacked on top of one another. Cases are similar to pallets but have sides to enclose the goods. Boxes are smaller containers made of wood, used for packaging and storage. Each type serves a specific purpose in the transportation and storage of goods, with pallets being the most commonly used for heavy loads and cases/boxes for smaller, more delicate items.</p></p>
