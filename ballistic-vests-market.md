<p><p>Ballistic vests are protective garments designed to absorb and reduce the impact of bullets or projectiles. The market is divided into two main types: soft ballistic vests and hard ballistic vests. Soft ballistic vests are made of flexible materials like Kevlar, offering comfort and mobility while providing protection against handgun fire. Hard ballistic vests, on the other hand, are composed of rigid materials like ceramic or metal plates, designed to provide higher level of protection against rifle fire. Both types cater to different levels of ballistic threats and user preferences.</p></p>
