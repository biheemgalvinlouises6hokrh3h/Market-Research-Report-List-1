<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The 4K Set Top Box (STB) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Internet Protocol Television (IPTV)</li><li>Satellite Television</li><li>Digital Terrestrial Television (DTT)</li><li>Internet Television Set-top Box (OTT)</li><li>Hybrid Television Set-top Box</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 4K Set Top Box (STB) market includes different types catering to various modes of content delivery. Internet Protocol Television (IPTV) STBs enable streaming of content over IP networks, while Satellite Television STBs receive signals from satellites. Digital Terrestrial Television (DTT) STBs pick up signals from broadcast towers. Internet Television Set-top Box (OTT) allows access to content over the internet. Hybrid Television Set-top Box combines features of multiple types, offering a wide range of content delivery options to consumers.</p></p>
