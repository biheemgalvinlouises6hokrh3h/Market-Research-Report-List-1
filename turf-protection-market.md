<p><p>Turf protection involves maintaining the health and integrity of grassy areas, such as lawns and sports fields. There are three main types of turf protection methods: biological, chemical, and mechanical. Biological methods involve using living organisms like beneficial bacteria or fungi to control pests and promote turf health. Chemical methods involve the use of pesticides and herbicides to prevent damage from pests and weeds. Mechanical methods involve physically protecting turf through techniques like aeration or installing protective barriers. Each method has its own benefits and potential drawbacks.</p></p>
