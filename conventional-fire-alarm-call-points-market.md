<p><p>In the conventional fire alarm call points market, there are two main types: glass element and plastic element. Glass element call points are designed with a breakable glass cover that must be shattered to activate the alarm. Plastic element call points, on the other hand, are equipped with a push-button mechanism for alarm activation. Both types serve the same purpose of providing a quick and easy way for individuals to manually trigger a fire alarm in case of an emergency.</p></p>
