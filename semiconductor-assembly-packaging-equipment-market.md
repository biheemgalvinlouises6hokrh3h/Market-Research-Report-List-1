<p><strong>The Semiconductor Assembly & Packaging Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Die Bonders</li><li>Wire Bonders</li><li>Packaging Equipment</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Semiconductor assembly and packaging equipment market can be categorized into Die Bonders, Wire Bonders, Packaging Equipment, and Others. Die bonders are used to attach the semiconductor die to the substrate. Wire bonders are used to connect the die to the package using wire bonding technology. Packaging equipment is used for the final encapsulation of the semiconductor device. Other equipment includes inspection systems, thermal management tools, and material handling systems. Each type plays a crucial role in the semiconductor assembly and packaging process.</p></p>
