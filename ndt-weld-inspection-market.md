<p><p>NDT weld inspection is a process used to assess the quality and integrity of welds. This market is divided into several types, including automated ultrasonic testing, which uses sound waves to detect defects; magnetic particle testing, where magnetic particles reveal surface cracks; radiographic testing, which uses X-rays or gamma rays to inspect internal welds; penetrant testing, which identifies surface flaws using dyes; visual testing, where inspectors visually assess welds; and other methods like eddy current testing and acoustic emission testing. Each method offers unique advantages and is used depending on the specific requirements of the weld inspection process.</p></p>
