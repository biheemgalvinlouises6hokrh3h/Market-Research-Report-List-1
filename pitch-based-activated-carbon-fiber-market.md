<p><p>Pitch-based activated carbon fiber is categorized into three main types: oil pitch, coal pitch, and others. Oil pitch is derived from petroleum products and is known for its high carbon content and good mechanical properties. Coal pitch is obtained from coal tar and is characterized by its excellent adsorption properties. The 'others' category includes pitches derived from various sources such as biomass or waste materials. Each type of pitch offers unique advantages and is used in different applications within the activated carbon fiber market.</p></p>
