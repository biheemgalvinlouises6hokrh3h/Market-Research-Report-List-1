<p><p>The PVC Paste Resin market offers two different types: the Micro Suspension Method and the Emulsion Method. The Micro Suspension Method involves preparing a PVC paste resin by suspending small PVC particles in a liquid medium to create a fine powder. On the other hand, the Emulsion Method involves creating a PVC paste resin by polymerizing vinyl chloride monomers in an aqueous emulsion. Both methods are used in the production of PVC paste resins, which have various applications in industries like construction, automotive, and packaging.</p></p>
