<p><p>The Automotive Exhaust Gas Recirculation System market is divided into two main types of EGR valves: Pneumatic EGR Valves and Electric EGR Valves. Pneumatic EGR Valves use air pressure to control the flow of exhaust gases back into the engine, while Electric EGR Valves utilize an electric motor for the same purpose. Both types of valves help reduce nitrogen oxide emissions and improve fuel efficiency in vehicles. The choice between pneumatic and electric valves depends on factors such as cost, performance, and ease of integration with the vehicle's electronic systems.</p></p>
