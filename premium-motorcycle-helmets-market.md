<p><p>Premium motorcycle helmets market is divided into two main types: Full Face Helmet and Open Face Helmet. Full Face Helmets provide complete coverage to the head and face, offering maximum protection in case of accidents. They are preferred by riders who prioritize safety. Open Face Helmets, also known as 3/4 helmets, cover the top, back, and sides of the head but leave the face open. They are popular among riders looking for a balance between protection and comfort, allowing for better ventilation and visibility.</p></p>
