<p><p>Thermal latent curing agents are used in two main types of markets: low temperature curing and high temperature curing. Low temperature curing agents are designed for use at temperatures below 120°C, making them ideal for applications where heat-sensitive materials are involved. High temperature curing agents, on the other hand, are used at temperatures above 120°C. These agents are typically used in applications where a higher degree of heat resistance is required. Both types of curing agents provide unique solutions for various industrial processes.</p></p>
