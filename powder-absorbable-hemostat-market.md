<p><p>Powder absorbable hemostats are used in surgical procedures to control bleeding. There are four main types of powder absorbable hemostats: gelatin-based, fibrin-based, cellulose-based, and others. Gelatin-based hemostats are derived from animal collagen and work by promoting blood clotting. Fibrin-based hemostats are made from human or animal plasma proteins and mimic the body's natural clotting mechanism. Cellulose-based hemostats are derived from plant fibers and provide a scaffold for clot formation. Others include synthetic and combination hemostats with unique properties.</p></p>
