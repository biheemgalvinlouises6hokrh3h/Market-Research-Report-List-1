<p><p>Sustainable surfactants are categorized into different types based on their composition and properties. Glycolipids, phospholipids, and fatty acids are derived from natural sources and have excellent biodegradability. Lipopeptides and lipoproteins are effective in reducing surface tension and forming stable emulsions. Polymeric surfactants have unique structures that offer enhanced stability and performance. Particulate biosurfactants consist of solid particles with surfactant properties, providing sustainable and efficient solutions for various applications in industries such as oil and gas, agriculture, and cosmetics.</p></p>
