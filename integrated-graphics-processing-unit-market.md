<p><p>The Integrated Graphics Processing Unit market can be categorized into two main types: Independent Integrated Graphics Processing Unit and Hybrid Integrated Graphics Processing Unit. Independent units are stand-alone GPUs, separate from the CPU, providing high performance for tasks like gaming and video editing. Hybrid units combine CPU and GPU functions into a single chip, offering a balance of performance and power efficiency. Both types cater to different consumer needs, with independent units focusing on high performance and hybrid units offering a more integrated solution.</p></p>
