<p><p>Commercial Electric Food Steamer Market Types can be classified into two main categories - Commercial Pressureless Electric Food Steamer and Commercial Pressure Electric Food Steamer. Pressureless electric food steamers use boiling water to create steam, while pressure electric food steamers use higher pressure to cook food faster. Pressureless steamers are suitable for gentle cooking of delicate foods, while pressure steamers are ideal for speeding up the cooking process and retaining nutrients in the food. Both types offer efficient and convenient cooking options for commercial kitchens.</p></p>
