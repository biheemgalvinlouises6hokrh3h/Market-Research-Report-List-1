<p><p>Autoclave inspection refers to the assessment of various components and systems within autoclaves to ensure their proper functioning and adherence to safety standards. The different types of inspections in this market include valves inspection, which involves checking and testing the valves for any leaks or malfunctions; heating coils inspection, which assesses the condition and performance of the heating coils; contactors inspection, which ensures the proper operation of electrical contactors; and steam traps inspection, which examines the steam traps for any issues that may affect the autoclave's efficiency.</p></p>
