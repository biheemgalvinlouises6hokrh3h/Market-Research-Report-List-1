BHKP is primarily made from hardwood fibers, offering high brightness and smoothness, suitable for printing and writing papers. On the other hand, BSKP is produced from softwood fibers, known for its strength and stiffness, commonly used in packaging papers. Both types of BKP have their own unique characteristics and applications in the paper industry.</p></p>
