<p><p>Structural Steel Market Types include various shapes such as I-Beams, which are known for their high strength and versatility in supporting heavy loads. Angle (L-Shape) steel is commonly used for framing, bracing, and support structures due to its perpendicular sides. Hollow Structural Section (HSS) Shape steel offers a lightweight yet durable option for architectural and construction applications. Z-Shape steel provides strength and stability in compression applications, while T-Shaped steel is often used for beams and columns in building construction.</p></p>
