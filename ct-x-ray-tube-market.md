<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The CT X-ray Tube Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Stationary Anode</li><li>Rotating Anode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CT X-ray Tube Market includes two main types: Stationary Anode and Rotating Anode tubes. Stationary Anode tubes have a fixed target on which electrons are focused to generate X-rays. They are commonly used in lower power CT machines. Rotating Anode tubes have a rotating target that allows for higher power X-ray generation and are commonly used in higher-end CT machines. The choice between these two types of tubes depends on the specific imaging requirements and power needs of the CT system.</p></p>
