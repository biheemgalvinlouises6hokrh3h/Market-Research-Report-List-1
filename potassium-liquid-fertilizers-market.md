<p><p>Potassium liquid fertilizers come in various types including potassium nitrate, sulphate of potash, and potassium chloride. Potassium nitrate is a water-soluble fertilizer that provides both potassium and nitrogen to plants. Sulphate of potash contains potassium and sulfur, making it suitable for crops with high sulfur requirements. Potassium chloride is a commonly used source of potassium in agriculture, although its high chlorine content may not be suitable for certain plants. Each type of potassium liquid fertilizer offers different benefits and may be suitable for different crops and soils.</p></p>
