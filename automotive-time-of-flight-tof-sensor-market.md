<p><strong>The Automotive Time of Flight ToF Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct ToF Sensors</li><li>Indirect ToF Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive Time of Flight (ToF) sensors are divided into two main types: Direct ToF sensors and Indirect ToF sensors. Direct ToF sensors measure the time it takes for light to travel to an object and reflect back, providing accurate distance measurements. Indirect ToF sensors use additional sensors or algorithms to calculate distance based on factors like phase shifts in the reflected light. Both types of ToF sensors play a crucial role in automotive applications for functions such as collision avoidance, parking assistance, and gesture recognition.</p></p>
