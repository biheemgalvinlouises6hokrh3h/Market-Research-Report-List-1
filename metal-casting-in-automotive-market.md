<p><strong>The Metal Casting in Automotive Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gravity Casting</li><li>High-Pressure Die Casting (HPDC)</li><li>Low-Pressure Die Casting (LPDC)</li><li>Sand Casting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Metal casting is a popular manufacturing process in the automotive industry, with various market types available. Gravity casting involves pouring molten metal into a mold. High-Pressure Die Casting (HPDC) uses high-pressure to force molten metal into a mold cavity. Low-Pressure Die Casting (LPDC) utilizes low pressure for the same purpose. Sand casting involves creating a mold by compacting sand around a pattern and pouring molten metal into it. These casting methods are crucial in producing automotive parts efficiently and cost-effectively.</p></p>
