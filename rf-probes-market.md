<p><ul><li>Passive Type</li><li>Active Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>RF Probes are essential tools in testing and measuring RF signals in electronic devices. The Passive Type of RF Probes rely solely on the signal being tested for power, making them simpler and more cost-effective. On the other hand, Active Type RF Probes require an external power source to operate, providing additional features such as signal amplification and filtering. Both types cater to different needs and preferences in the market, with Passive Type being more straightforward and economical, and Active Type offering more advanced capabilities.</p></p>
