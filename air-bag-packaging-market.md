<p><p>The air bag packaging market comprises various types of packaging materials designed to protect goods during transport and storage. Bubble wraps consist of small air-filled bubbles sealed between two layers of plastic film. Inflated packaging bags are filled with air and act as protective cushions around items. Air pillows are lightweight and versatile, ideal for filling empty spaces and voids. Lastly, other types of air bag packaging include foam-filled bags, inflatable packaging tubes, and honeycomb matrix systems. Each type aims to provide cushioning and shock absorption to prevent damage to goods.</p></p>
