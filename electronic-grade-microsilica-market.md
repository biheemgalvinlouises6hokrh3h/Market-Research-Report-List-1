<p><ul><li>Angular</li><li>Spherical</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electronic grade microsilica is available in two main types: angular and spherical. Angular microsilica particles have irregular shapes with sharp edges, providing high packing density and good mechanical properties in applications requiring improved strength and durability. Spherical microsilica particles have a smoother surface and increased flowability, making them ideal for applications requiring high dispersion and uniformity, such as in electronic components. Both types offer unique advantages depending on the specific requirements of the application, allowing for tailored performance characteristics.</p></p>
