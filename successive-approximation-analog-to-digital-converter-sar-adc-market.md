<p><p>Successive Approximation Analog-to-Digital Converter (SAR ADC) is available in three main market types: Single Channel, Duel Channels, and Four Channels. The Single Channel SAR ADC is designed to convert one analog input channel to a digital output, making it suitable for applications with only one input channel. The Duel Channels SAR ADC can convert two analog input channels simultaneously, providing flexibility for applications requiring two input channels. The Four Channels SAR ADC offers the capability to convert four analog input channels concurrently, making it ideal for applications with multiple input channels.</p></p>
