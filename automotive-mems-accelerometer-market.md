<p><p>Automotive MEMS accelerometers are vital sensors used in vehicles for various applications. The market types include piezoelectric accelerometers, which use piezoelectric crystals to measure changes in electrical charge due to applied force; capacitive accelerometers, which measure changes in capacitance when subjected to acceleration; and thermal induction accelerometers, which rely on changes in temperature to detect motion. Each type offers unique benefits and applications in automotive systems, contributing to the overall functionality and safety of vehicles.</p></p>
