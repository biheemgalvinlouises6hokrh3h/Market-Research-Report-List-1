<p><strong>The Aquaculture Feed Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mash Feed</li><li>Pellet Feed</li><li>Expanded Feed</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Aquaculture feed market offers three main types of products: Mash feed, Pellet feed, and Expanded feed. Mash feed consists of finely ground ingredients that are mixed together into a meal, often used for small fish or shrimp. Pellet feed is formed by compacting the ingredients into small pellets, making it easier for fish to eat. Expanded feed is made by expanding the ingredients through high heat and pressure, creating a floating feed that is ideal for many fish species. Each type serves specific purposes depending on the size and species of the aquatic animals being fed.</p></p>
