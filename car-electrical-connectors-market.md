Wire to Wire Connector refers to connectors that join two separate wires together. Wire to Board Connector connects wires to a circuit board. Board to Board Connector connects two circuit boards. These connectors play a crucial role in ensuring proper electrical connections within a car, allowing for seamless communication between various components and systems.</p></p>
