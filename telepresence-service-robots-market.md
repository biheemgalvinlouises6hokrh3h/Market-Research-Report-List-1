<p><p>Telepresence service robots are mainly categorized into two types: Mobile telepresence robots and stationary telepresence robots. </p><p>Mobile telepresence robots are designed to move around and interact in different environments, providing a sense of presence to users remotely. These robots are equipped with wheels or legs for mobility.</p><p>Stationary telepresence robots, on the other hand, are fixed in one location but can be remotely controlled to move their heads or camera to observe surroundings. They are often used for video conferencing or remote monitoring applications.</p></p>
