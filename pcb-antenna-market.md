<p><p>PCB antenna market includes various types such as Inverted-F Antenna (IFA), Microstrip Inverted-F Antenna (MIFA), and others. IFA antennas have a simple structure with good radiation efficiency, making them a popular choice for various wireless applications. MIFA antennas are a compact version of IFAs with improved performance in terms of bandwidth and gain. Other types of PCB antennas may include patch antennas, monopole antennas, or dipole antennas, each offering different advantages based on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
