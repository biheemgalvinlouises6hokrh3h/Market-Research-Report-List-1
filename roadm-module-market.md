<p><p>ROADM modules include various types like Wavelength Selective Switch (WSS), Optical Channel Monitoring (OCM), and Variable Optical Attenuators (VOAs). Wavelength Selective Switches enable dynamic wavelength routing, Optical Channel Monitoring provides real-time performance monitoring, and Variable Optical Attenuators adjust the signal power levels. These components play crucial roles in network flexibility, efficiency, and reliability in optical communication systems. The market for ROADM modules is driven by growing demand for high-capacity networks and advanced optical technologies to meet the increasing data traffic requirements.</p></p>
