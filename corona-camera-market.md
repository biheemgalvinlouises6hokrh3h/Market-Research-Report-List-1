<p><ul><li>Ultraviolet</li><li>Multi-spectral</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Corona cameras come in different types, including ultraviolet and multi-spectral. Ultraviolet cameras use UV light to detect corona discharge, which is not visible to the naked eye. These cameras are highly sensitive and ideal for identifying electrical faults at an early stage. On the other hand, multi-spectral cameras utilize multiple wavelengths to provide a more comprehensive analysis of corona discharge. They can detect different types of corona activity and offer a more detailed view of the electrical system's health.</p></p>
