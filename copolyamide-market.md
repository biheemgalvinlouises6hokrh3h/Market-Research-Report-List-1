<p><p>Copolyamide market is categorized into two main types: Granular and Powder. Granular copolyamide is in solid form with larger particles whereas powder copolyamides are finely ground into a powder form. Granular copolyamides are preferred for applications requiring uniform melting and thermal stability, such as injection molding and extrusion. On the other hand, powder copolyamides are commonly used in powder coating applications due to their ease of dispersibility and enhanced adhesion properties. Both types cater to a wide range of industries such as automotive, electronics, and consumer goods.</p></p>
