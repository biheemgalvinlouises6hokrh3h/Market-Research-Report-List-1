<p><p>Harmonic gear drives come in three main types: cup style, hat style, and pancake style. Cup style gear drives are compact and have high torque capacity, making them suitable for applications where space is limited. Hat style gear drives have a flatter design, making them ideal for applications where height is a concern. Pancake style gear drives have a flat, disc-shaped form factor, providing high torque capacity and low backlash. Each type of gear drive offers unique benefits depending on the specific application requirements.</p></p>
