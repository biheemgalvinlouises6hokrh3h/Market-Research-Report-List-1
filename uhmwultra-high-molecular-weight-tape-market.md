<p><p>UHMW (Ultra-high Molecular Weight) Tape is available in two main types: Single-sided Tape and Double-sided Tape. Single-sided Tape has adhesive on only one side, typically used for sealing applications or attaching materials to surfaces. Double-sided Tape has adhesive on both sides, allowing for bonding two surfaces together. Both types of UHMW Tape provide strong adhesion and are commonly used for applications requiring high durability, low friction, and chemical resistance. They are popular choices in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing.</p></p>
