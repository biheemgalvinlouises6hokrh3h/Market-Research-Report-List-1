<p><p>Long fiber composites market can be categorized into different types based on the type of polymers used, such as PP (polypropylene), PA (polyamide), PEEK (polyetheretherketone), PPA (polyphthalamide), and other polymers. Each type of polymer offers unique characteristics and benefits in terms of strength, durability, and chemical resistance. PP composites are known for their lightweight and cost-effective nature, while PEEK composites are recognized for their high temperature resistance. PA and PPA composites are valued for their excellent mechanical properties and chemical resistance.</p></p>
