<p><ul><li>Singlemode</li><li>Multimode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>High density fiber optic cables are available in two main types: singlemode and multimode. Singlemode cables are designed for long-distance communication and use a single pathway for light transmission, making them ideal for applications requiring high bandwidth over long distances. On the other hand, multimode cables are better suited for shorter distances and use multiple pathways for light transmission, allowing for easier installation and maintenance in local area networks. Both types of cables offer high-density solutions for data transmission in various industries.</p></p>
